AS INTRODUCED  
 
An Act relating to mental health; creating the  
Oklahoma Commission on Aging with Serious Mental 
Illness; stating purpose; specifying membership of 
Commission ; requiring publication of report by 
certain date; providing for travel reimbursement; 
requiring compliance with Oklahoma Open Meeting  Act; 
authorizing staff assistance;  providing for 
codification ; and declaring an  emergency.  
 
 
 
 
 
BE IT ENACTED BY THE PEOPLE OF THE STATE OF OKLAHOMA:  
SECTION 1.     NEW LAW     A new section of law to be codified 
in the Oklahoma Statutes as Section 3-114 of Title 43A, unless there 
is created a duplication in numbering, reads as follows:  
A.  There is hereby created the Oklahoma Commission on Aging 
with Serious Mental Illness.  The purpose of the Commission shall be 
to study, evaluate and make recommend ations for any changes to state 
policy, rules or statutes to more efficiently and effectively serve

B.  The Commission shall publish a report of its findings no 
later than June 1, 2019, the date on which members ' terms shall end.  
C.  Members of the Commi ssion shall serve at the pleasure of the 
appointing authority.  Vacancies in a position shall be filled in 
the same manner as the original appointment.  
D.  Travel reimbursement for members of the Commi ssion who are 
legislators shall be made pursuant to Section 456 of Title 74 of t he 
Oklahoma Statutes , and travel reimbursement for members of the 
Commission who are state employees shall be reimbursed by the Offi ce 
of Management and Enterprise  Services.  Travel reimbursement for 
other members of the Committee shall be made by the resp ective 
appointing authorities pursuant to the State Travel Reimbursement 
Act. 
E.  Proceedings of all meetings of the Commission shall comply 
with the provisions of the Oklahoma Open Meeting  Act.

SECTION 2.  It being immediately necessary for the preservation 
of the public peace, health or safety, an emergency is hereby 
declared to exist, by reason whereof this act shall take effect and 
be in full force from and after its passage and approval.
