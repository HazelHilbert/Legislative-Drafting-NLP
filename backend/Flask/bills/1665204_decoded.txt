A   R E S O L U T I O N
Honoring Carli Terepka as a 2017 IFBB North American  
Champion.
WHEREAS, The members of the Senate of the 132nd General  
Assembly of Ohio are pleased to congratulate Carli Terepka on  
placing first in the women’s physique class B category at the 2017  
International Federation of Bodybuilders North American  
Championship; and
WHEREAS, Carli Terepka has combined talent and competitive  
spirit with the highest ideals of good sportsmanship to establish  
herself as a truly exceptional athlete. With admirable dedication,  
she has worked diligently toward attaining ultimate proficiency in  
bodybuilding, and her victory in the women’s physique class B  
category at this year’s International Federation of Bodybuilders  
North American Championship is a fitting reward for her efforts.  
Her unwavering commitment to excellence has earned her the respect  
and esteem of many, and she is certainly deserving of high praise;  
and
WHEREAS, Carli Terepka’s accomplishments and leadership, both  
in and out of competition, are a justifiable source of pride and  
an outstanding reflection not only on her but also on her family,  
her coach, Nikki Gutierrez, her gym, UXL Fitness 24/7 Access, and  
her community. Indeed, the same maturity and experience she has  
gained through her participation in sports will greatly aid her in  
meeting the challenges of the future; and
WHEREAS, With athletic competition playing such an important  
part in the establishment of role models for our young people, we  
are extremely pleased to pay tribute to Carli Terepka on her  
exemplary success. Throughout intense training and preparation,  
she has consistently demonstrated the talent, integrity, and  1
