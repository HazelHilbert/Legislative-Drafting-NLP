AN ACT WAIVING INITIAL OCCUPATIONAL LICENSING FEES FOR CERTAIN INDIVIDUALS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General Assembly convened:
That the general statutes be amended to waive initial occupational licensing fees for low-income individuals, individuals under twenty-five years of age and veterans.
Statement of Purpose:  
To waive initial occupational licensing fees for low-income individuals, individuals under twenty-five years of age and veterans.
